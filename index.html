About RWC

The Royal Westerns Club has been actively participating in various sports matches since its inception. Our teams have competed in local and regional tournaments, showcasing talent, determination, and sportsmanship. We have a proud history of achievements and are continually striving to improve our performance and reach new heights in the world of sports.

Our club, founded by seven dedicated members, has grown exponentially since its registration on September 15, 2015. With a strong foundation based on teamwork, our club has expanded its activities to include not only sports but also community-building initiatives. We believe in creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and can contribute to our collective success.

Training at the Royal Westerns Club is a cornerstone of our philosophy. We offer rigorous training programs designed to enhance the skills and fitness levels of our members. Our experienced coaches and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that every member receives the best possible preparation, whether they are competing in sports or participating in community activities.

Welcome to the Royal Westerns Club. The Royal Westerns Club was officially registered with Customer Affairs Victoria on September 15, 2015. Our club is proudly rooted in the vibrant and diverse western region of Melbourne. We are a community of sport-loving individuals, primarily Nepali students, who share a passion for teamwork, sportsmanship, and community building.
